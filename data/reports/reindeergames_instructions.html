RG Channel — Instructions

How to post proofs and backblasts for EC / Hardsh!t / CSAUP / Popups / Donations / Challenge Flag

EC / Hardsh!t / csaup

0

Take a photo of the PAX who ran together and the proof on your tracker app.

Only one PAX needs to enter the backblast — but make sure someone does it and includes your name or you won't get credit.

1

Go to the appropriate rg_ channel for the activity:

rg_ec1 (if you did 1)
rg_ec2 (if you did 2)
rg_ec3 (if you did 3)
rg_hardshit (finished a half marathon)
rg_csaup (team completed a 1stF approved csaup)
2

Type /backblast and click send.

3

Fill out these portions of the backblast:

  • Enter a Title
  • Leave the AO as is — don’t change it.
  • Enter the Q (that's you!)
  • Select the PAX (all who participated)
4

Click Submit.

5

Reply in the thread to your backblast.

6

List who you EC’d with and include the two pictures:

Photo of the PAX
Photo of the tracker / proof

Popup!

0

Take a picture or video as proof of completion (game, soup dump, whatever).

1

Go to the rg_popup channel.

2

Type /backblast and click send.

3

Fill out these portions:

  • Enter a Title
  • Leave the AO as is — don’t change it.
  • Enter the Q (you!)
  • Select the PAX (all who participated or just you)
4

Click Submit.

5

Reply in the thread to your backblast.

6

List the PAX (might be just you!) and include the proof (photo/video).

Donation

0

Take a picture of proof of your minimum $25 donation (screenshot or photo).

1

Go to the rg_3rdf_donation channel.

2

Type /backblast and click send.

3

Fill out these portions:

  • Enter a Title
  • Leave the AO as is — don’t change it.
  • Enter the Q (you!)
  • Select the PAX (probably just you and your donation)
  • Scroll to the bottom and enter the total Units (e.g. $50) for total donations.
4

Click Submit.

5

Reply in the thread to your backblast.

6

List the PAX (might be just you!) and include the proof. If you're posting multiple pax, show proof for each pax's donation.

Challenge Flag

0

Take a picture of you getting the challenge flag.

1

Go to the rg_challenge_flag channel.

2

Type /backblast and click send.

3

Fill out these portions:

  • Enter a Title
  • Leave the AO as is — don’t change it.
  • Enter the Q (you!)
  • Select the PAX (just you)
4

Click Submit.

5

Reply in the thread to your backblast and include the picture. (You can also put the single picture directly in the backblast if you prefer.)

You get one point for each day you keep and post the flag until someone else wins it. You do not need to enter a backblast every day — only on the day you first win the flag. But you must post the flag each day or you'll get negative points.

Tip: keep your photos clear and timestamped (screenshot of tracker apps often works best). If you have questions about which channel to use, ask a leader or the Q for the post.
